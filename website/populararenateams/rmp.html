 The 3v3 team consisting of a rogue, mage, and priest, or better known as RMP, rose to power in the Burning Crusade expansion and remained popular due to the immense crowd control the team posesses. Their strategy relies on crowd controlling a healer long enough to score a kill on one of the other players.
