PvP rewards or PvP gear is gear (armor/weapons/trinkets, etc) designed for Player vs. Player (PvP) combat. PvP gear can be purchased using either [Honor Points] or  [Conquest Points]. Some older gear can be purchased using specific PvP tokens awarded by completing outdoor PvP objectives.

PvP gear provides superior stats and bonuses for PvP combat, and is the optimal gearing choice for engaging in PvP. PvP gear serves as the primary form of progression for players in PvP, with acquiring a set of competitive PvP gear significantly improving a player's performance in this type of play. In comparison, PvP gear is sub-optimal for engaging in PvE content.
