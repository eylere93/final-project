Conquest Points are the upper-level currency introduced with Cataclysm and rewarded for participating in PvP encounters in the form of rated battlegrounds and ranked arenacombat. The lower-level PvP currency, rewarded from non-rated battlegrounds and world PvP, is awarded in the form of Honor points.
